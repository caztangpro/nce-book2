📦 英语量词速查

Measure Words / Partitives — 让不可数名词"可数化"

核心原理：英语不可数名词不能直接加 a/an（不能说 a water, a bread），需要借助量词来表达"一个/一块/一杯…"
最万能的量词是 a piece of，几乎覆盖所有情况。

🍞 形状 · 块状

量词含义搭配
a piece of一块/片/张/条/则cheese, paper, news, advice, music, cake, furniture, land
a slice of薄片bread, cake, pizza, ham, cheese, lemon
a loaf of一整条（面包）bread
a bar of条状/块状chocolate, soap, gold
a block of方块ice, wood, flats, cheese
a sheet of薄片/张paper, metal, glass
a lump of不规则块coal, sugar, clay
a strip of长条land, paper, cloth
a cube of小方块sugar, ice
a grain of颗粒rice, sand, salt, truth

课文 L6：a piece of cheese

📄 抽象名词量词

量词搭配
a piece ofadvice, news, information, research, work
a bit ofluck, fun, trouble, money（口语）

a fit ofanger, laughter, jealousy（一时冲动）
a sense ofhumour, pride, achievement, direction
⚠️ 注意：a pair of 的谓语搭配
This pair of shoes is expensive. ✅（pair 是单数 → is）
These shoes are expensive. ✅（复数名词）
A pair of glasses costs a lot. ✅（pair 作主语时动词用单数）
💡 技巧：实在想不起来具体量词时，用 a piece of 几乎永远正确！
a piece of bread = a slice of bread ✅（piece 更通用）
a piece of chocolate = a bar of chocolate ✅
a piece of news ✅（唯一选择）
