📜 课文 · Text

先读一遍英文。不认识的地方别查词典，尝试从上下文猜测。

A private conversation

Last week I went to the theatre. I had a very good seat. The play was very interesting. I did not enjoy it. A young man and a young woman were sitting behind me. They were talking loudly. I got very angry. I could not hear the actors. I turned round. I looked at the man and the woman angrily. They did not pay any attention. In the end, I could not bear it. I turned round again. 'I can't hear a word!' I said angrily.

'It's none of your business,' the young man said rudely. 'This is a private conversation!'

私人谈话

上星期我去看戏了。我的座位非常好。那部剧非常有趣。但我却无法欣赏。一男青年和一女青年正坐在我的后面。他们大声地说着话。我非常生气。我听不到演员在说什么。我转过头来。我生气地看着那一男一女。他们毫不理睬。最后，我实在忍无可忍了。我又一次转过头来。"我一个字也听不见了！"我生气地说。

"不关你的事，"那男青年粗鲁地说。"这是私人谈话！"

💡 注意：angry（形容词）→ angrily（副词）。英语中常在形容词后加 -ly 构成副词。第二册中你会频繁遇到这种现象。

  1. Where did the writer go last week?
  2. Why didn't he enjoy the play?
  3. What did the young man say to the writer?
✏️ 练习 1：在横线上写下你的回答

参考答案：
1. The writer went to the theatre last week.
2. He did not enjoy it because a young man and a young woman were sitting behind him and talking loudly.
3. The young man said: "It's none of your business. This is a private conversation!"

🔑 关键句型 · Key Structure

简单陈述句的语序 (Word Order in Simple Statements)

英语简单陈述句的基本语序是：

主语 (Subject) + 动词 (Verb) + 宾语 (Object) + 状语 (Adverbial)

从课文中的例子：

主语 动词 宾语 状语
I went — to the theatre
I had a very good seat —
The play was very interesting —
They were talking — loudly

词序规则

  • 主语总是在句首（名词或代词）
  • 动词紧跟在主语之后（系动词/不及物/及物）
  • 宾语在及物动词之后（如 have a seat）
  • 状语通常在宾语之后（地点 → 方式 → 时间）
💡 中文说"我昨天去剧院了"→ 时间状语"昨天"在前。
但英文说 I went to the theatre yesterday. → 时间状语在句尾。
这是中英文最显著的区别之一，写作时务必注意！

  2. a good / had / we / time / very
  3. the window / opened / he / quietly
  4. a letter / my mother / wrote / me / to

  2. 他们正在大声说话。

🔍 特殊难点 · Special Difficulties

1. angry vs angrily（形容词 vs 副词）

  • 形容词修饰名词：an angry man（一个生气的男人）
  • 副词修饰动词：He spoke angrily（他生气地说）

2. pay attention（注意 / 理会）

固定搭配：pay attention to something

例句：They did not pay any attention to me.

3. go to the theatre（去看戏）

类似表达：go to the cinema（去看电影）、go to the concert（去听音乐会）

注意：go to the theatre 不是"去剧院"(去工作)，而是"去看戏"(作为观众)。
