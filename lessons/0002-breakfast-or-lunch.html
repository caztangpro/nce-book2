Lesson 2: Breakfast or Lunch?

📖 关键句型：现在进行时 vs 一般现在时  |  📝 第 2 课 / 共 96 课

📜 课文 · Text

It was Sunday. I never get up early on Sundays. I sometimes stay in bed until lunchtime. Last Sunday I got up very late. I looked out of the window. It was dark outside. 'What a day!' I thought. 'It's raining again.' Just then, the telephone rang. It was my aunt Lucy. 'I've just arrived by train,' she said. 'I'm coming to see you.'

'But I'm still having breakfast,' I said.

'What are you doing?' she asked.

'I'm having breakfast,' I repeated.

'Dear me!' she said. 'Do you always get up so late? It's one o'clock!'

那是个星期天。我从不在星期天早起。我有时一直躺到午饭时间。上个星期天我起得很晚。我望出窗外。外面一片漆黑。"什么鬼天气！"我想。"又下雨了。"正在这时，电话铃响了。是我姑母露西打来的。"我刚下火车，"她说。"我正要来看你。"

"但我还在吃早饭呢，"我说。

"你在干什么？"她问道。

"我在吃早饭，"我又说了一遍。

"天哪！"她说。"你总是起得这么晚吗？现在都一点了！"

📝 词汇 · Vocabulary

outside /ˌaʊtˈsaɪd/ adv. 外面
ring /rɪŋ/ v. 响铃 (rang, rung)
aunt /ɑːnt/ n. 姑母，姨母
repeat /rɪˈpiːt/ v. 重复

对比：一般现在时 vs 现在进行时

一般现在时现在进行时
用法习惯、常态、客观事实正在发生的事
频率副词：always, usually, sometimes, never时间标志：now, at the moment, still, Listen!

从课文中对比

一般现在时（习惯）:

  • I never get up early on Sundays. （每个周日都如此 — 习惯）
  • I sometimes stay in bed until lunchtime. （有时如此 — 习惯）
  • Do you always get up so late? （问一贯的习惯）

现在进行时（此刻正在做）:

  • It is raining again. （说话时正在下雨）
  • I'm coming to see you. （即将发生的行动）
  • I am still having breakfast. （此时此刻正在吃）
  • What are you doing? （问此刻在做什么）

本课最精彩的部分

课文的"笑点"在于：作者说 "I'm still having breakfast"（我正在吃早饭），而 Aunt Lucy 惊呼 "It's one o'clock! Do you always get up so late?"（都下午一点了！你总是起这么晚吗？）

  • 一般现在时 Do you always get up so late? — 问的是一个习惯（你这个人是不是一直都这样？）
  • 现在进行时 I'm having breakfast. — 说的是此刻的动作（我一点钟了还在吃早饭）

✏️ 练习

练习 1：选择正确的时态
  1. She usually _________ (get up / gets up / is getting up) at 7 o'clock.
  2. Listen! Someone _________ (knocks / is knocking) at the door.
  3. I _________ (never eat / am never eating) meat. I'm a vegetarian.
  4. He _________ (still sleeps / is still sleeping). Don't wake him up.
  5. What _________ you _________ (do / are ... doing) right now?
  6. The sun _________ (rises / is rising) in the east.
答案：
1. gets up（习惯）
2. is knocking（正在发生，Listen! 是标志词）
3. never eat（习惯，频率副词 never）
4. is still sleeping（此刻仍在进行，still 是标志词）
5. are ... doing（right now = 此刻）
6. rises（客观真理，太阳东升）
练习 2：用动词的正确形式填空

用括号里动词的 一般现在时 或 现在进行时 填空：

  1. A: Where's John? B: He _________ (have) a shower.
  2. A: What _________ you _________ (do) in your free time? B: I _________ (read) books.
  3. She _________ (work) in a hospital. She's a nurse.
  4. Be quiet! I _________ (try) to concentrate.
  5. We _________ (go) to the beach every summer.
答案：
1. is having（此刻正在洗澡）
2. do ... do / read（空闲时间做的事 = 习惯）
3. works（职业 = 常态）
4. am trying（正在尝试集中注意力）
5. go（every summer = 每年的习惯）
练习 3：翻译句子（英译中）
  1. I never get up early at weekends.
  2. What are you doing at the moment?
  3. She sometimes stays in bed until 10 o'clock.
  4. Listen! The telephone is ringing.
  5. 'Dear me!' she said. 'Do you always eat so fast?'
参考答案：
1. 我周末从不早起。
2. 你现在在做什么？
3. 她有时在床上躺到十点钟。
4. 听！电话铃在响。
5. "天哪！"她说。"你总是吃得这么快吗？"

🔍 特殊难点

1. on Sundays vs last Sunday

  • on Sundays = 每个周日（泛指）→ 用一般现在时
  • last Sunday = 上周日（特指）→ 用一般过去时

课文对比：I never get up early on Sundays. vs Last Sunday I got up very late.

2. until（直到…为止）

  • I stayed in bed until lunchtime. （我一直躺在床上，直到午饭时间）
  • Wait here until I come back. （在这儿等到我回来）

3. What a day!（感叹句）

结构：What + (a/an) + 名词 + 主语 + 动词!

  • What a beautiful day!
  • What a nice surprise!
  • What interesting books!（可数复数不加 a/an）

🎤 口语练习

用英文回答以下问题（大声说出来！）
  1. Do you always get up early?
  2. What are you doing right now?（除了回答这个问题之外 😄）
  3. What do you usually do on Sundays?
  4. What is the weather like outside at the moment?

💡 回答时要交替使用一般现在时（习惯）和现在进行时（此刻）。

📌 本课要点回顾

  • ✅ 课文：周日赖床被姑母"抓包"的幽默故事
  • ✅ 现在进行时 be + doing → 此刻正在发生的动作
  • ✅ 一般现在时 do/does → 习惯、常态、客观事实
  • ✅ 关键词：still, at the moment, now, Listen! → 现在进行时
  • ✅ 关键词：always, usually, sometimes, never, every → 一般现在时
