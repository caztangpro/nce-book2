Lesson 11: One Good Turn Deserves Another

📜 课文 · Text

One good turn deserves another

I was having dinner at a restaurant when Tony Steele came in. Tony worked in a lawyer's office years ago, but he is now working at a bank. He gets a good salary, but he always borrows money from his friends and never pays it back. Tony saw me and came and sat at the same table. He has never borrowed money from me. While he was eating, I asked him to lend me twenty pounds. To my surprise, he gave me the money immediately. 'I have never borrowed any money from you,' Tony said, 'so now you can pay for my dinner!'

善有善报

我正在一家餐馆吃饭，这时托尼·斯蒂尔走了进来。托尼多年前在一家律师事务所工作，但现在他在一家银行上班。他薪水不错，但他总是向朋友借钱，而且从不归还。托尼看到了我，过来在同一张桌旁坐下。他从未向我借过钱。他正在吃饭时，我请他借给我二十英镑。令我吃惊的是，他立刻把钱给了我。"我从未向你借过钱，"托尼说，"所以现在你可以付我的晚餐钱！"

🔑 关键句型：间接宾语 (Indirect Object) 结构

什么是双宾语结构？

有些动词（如 give, lend, pay, borrow, buy, tell）可以跟两个宾语：

  • 间接宾语 (IO) → 动作的接收者（通常是"人"）
  • 直接宾语 (DO) → 动作的直接承受者（通常是"物"）
句型 A：动词 + IO + DO
He gave me the money.
IO（人）在前，DO（物）在后
课文中：
He gave me the money immediately.
I asked him to lend me twenty pounds.
句型 B：动词 + DO + to/for + IO
He gave the money to me.
DO（物）在前，to/for + 人 在后
改写：
He gave the money to me.
Please lend twenty pounds to me.

什么时候用 to？什么时候用 for？

介词含义常见动词例子
to "给" → 方向性 give, lend, pay, send, tell, show, offer I gave the book to him.
for "为" → 目的性 buy, make, cook, get, find, choose I bought a gift for you.

课文中 to 的用法的实际例子：

  • Tony worked in a lawyer's office.（in 表地点）
  • He is now working at a bank.（at 表具体机构）
  • He borrows money from his friends.（from 表来源——借"入"）
  • pay for my dinner（for 表"为……付钱"）
  • lend money to someone（借"出"给人）

borrow vs lend — 借入 vs 借出

borrow（借入）lend（借出）
方向 ← 从别人那里借进来 → 把自己的借给别人
结构 borrow + 物 + from + 人 lend + 人 + 物 / lend + 物 + to + 人
例子 I borrowed £20 from him. He lent me £20.
💡 记忆法：
borrow = bring + → "带进来" → 借入
lend = leave + → "拿出去" → 借出
或者想中文：borrow = 借，lend = 贷

pay back vs pay for

短语含义例子
pay back还（钱）He never pays it back.（他从不还钱）
pay for为……付账You can pay for my dinner.（你可以付我的晚餐）
pay + 人 + 钱付给某人多少钱I paid him £10.

ask sb to do sth

课文中：I asked him to lend me twenty pounds.

结构：ask + 人 + to + 动词原形

类似用法的动词：

  • tell sb to do sth — 告诉某人做某事
  • want sb to do sth — 想要某人做某事
  • invite sb to do sth — 邀请某人做某事
  • teach sb to do sth — 教某人做某事

✏️ 练习

练习 1：用 borrow 或 lend 填空
  1. Can I _______________ your pen, please?
  2. He _______________ me his bike for the weekend.
  3. She _______________ some money from her mother.
  4. Could you _______________ me your umbrella? It's raining.
  5. I don't like to _______________ money from friends.
答案：
1. borrow（借用"进来"）
2. lent（借出给他——过去时）
3. borrowed（从妈妈那里借来）
4. lend（借出给我）
5. borrow（从朋友那里借入）
练习 2：用 to 或 for 填空
  1. He gave the money _______ me.
  2. She bought a gift _______ her friend.
  3. Please lend your dictionary _______ Tom.
  4. I made a cup of tea _______ my mother.
  5. Could you pay the bill _______ me?
答案：
1. to（give sth to sb）
2. for（buy sth for sb）
3. to（lend sth to sb）
4. for（make sth for sb）
5. for（pay for sb——为某人付账）
练习 3：改写句子（IO + DO ↔ DO + to/for + IO）
  1. He gave me the book. → He gave _______________
  2. She lent him her car. → She lent _______________
  3. I bought my mother a present. → I bought _______________
  4. Please pass me the salt. → Please pass _______________
  5. He told us a story. → He told _______________
答案：
1. the book to me.
2. her car to him.
3. a present for my mother.
4. the salt to me.
5. a story to us.
练习 4：中译英
  1. 他立刻把钱给了我。
  2. 你能借给我二十英镑吗？
  3. 他从不还钱。
  4. 我请他借给我一些钱。
  5. 你可以付我的晚餐钱。
参考答案：
1. He gave me the money immediately.
2. Can you lend me twenty pounds?
3. He never pays it back.
4. I asked him to lend me some money.
5. You can pay for my dinner.

🔍 特殊难点

1. 标题谚语：One good turn deserves another

字面：一个好行为值得另一个好行为
含义：善有善报 / 以德报德

类似的英语谚语：

  • One hand washes the other.（互相帮助）
  • What goes around comes around.（善恶终有报）
  • You scratch my back, I'll scratch yours.（你帮我我帮你）

2. pay 的用法

搭配含义例子
pay + 钱付多少钱I paid £20.
pay + 人付钱给某人I paid him.
pay for + 物为某物付钱pay for the dinner
pay back还钱pay back the money

3. while 的用法复习

课文：While he was eating, I asked him to lend me twenty pounds.

  • while + 进行时 →"在……期间"
  • 复习自 Lesson 7：while 表示"两个动作同时发生"

4. 现在完成时的复习

课文中出现了两次现在完成时：

  • He has never borrowed money from me.（从未借过，从过去到现在）
  • I have never borrowed any money from you.（同上）
