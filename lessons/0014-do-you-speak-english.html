Lesson 14: Do You Speak English?

📜 课文 · Text

Do You Speak English?

I had an amusing experience last year. After I had left a small village in the south of France, I drove on to the next town. On the way, a young man waved to me. I stopped and he asked me for a lift. As soon as he had got into the car, I said good morning to him in French. He replied in the same language. Apart from a few words, I do not know any French at all. Neither of us spoke during the journey. I had nearly reached the town, when the young man suddenly said, very slowly, "Do you speak English?" As I soon learnt, he was English himself!

你会说英语吗？

去年我有过一次有趣的经历。我离开法国南部的一个小村庄后，继续驶往下一个城镇。途中，一个年轻人向我招手。我停下来，他请求搭车。他一上车，我就用法语向他问早上好。他也用法语回答我。除了个别单词，我根本不会法语。旅途中我们谁也没说话。快要到镇上时，那个年轻人突然慢慢地问道："你会说英语吗？"我很快就知道了，他本人就是英国人！

🔑 关键句型：过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)

什么是过去完成时？

过去完成时 (had + 过去分词) 表示"过去的过去"——在过去的某个时间之前已经完成的动作。

🚗 离开村庄
had left
（更早）
→ 然后 →
🛣️ 驶向下一城镇
drove on
（之后）
→ 现在 →
📖 讲故事
现在时

关键：两个动作都发生在过去，过去完成时告诉读者"哪个更早发生"。

结构公式

句型结构例子
肯定句主语 + had + 过去分词I had left the village.
否定句主语 + had not (hadn't) + 过去分词I hadn't left yet.
疑问句Had + 主语 + 过去分词?Had you left the village?

课文中使用过去完成时的句子

  1. After I had left a small village … I drove on to the next town.
    先离开村庄 → 后开车去下一城
  2. As soon as he had got into the car, I said good morning.
    先上车 → 后问候
  3. I had nearly reached the town, when the young man suddenly said …
    "几乎已经到达时"——用过去完成时强调"即将完成的状态"
💡 课文为什么不用过去完成时的地方？
waved / stopped / asked / replied / spoke / said / learnt 都用一般过去时——因为这些动作是按时间顺序描述的，不需要强调"更早"。
规则：只有需要强调"某个过去动作之前"的时候，才用过去完成时。

过去完成时 vs 现在完成时 vs 一般过去时

过去完成时 (had done)现在完成时 (have done)一般过去时 (did)
参考时间 以过去某时间为基准 以现在为基准 单纯的过去事实
含义 "过去的过去" "从过去到现在" "过去某时发生过"
例子 He had left when I arrived.
我到时他已经走了		 He has left. (= He is not here.)
他已经走了（现在不在了）		 He left yesterday.
他昨天走了

典型搭配：过去完成时的时间标志

搭配含义课文例子
after + 过去完成时在……之后After I had left…
as soon as + 过去完成时一……就……As soon as he had got into the car…
had nearly / almost … when几乎已经……这时突然I had nearly reached the town, when…
before + 过去完成时在……之前（已经）He had left before I arrived.
by the time + 过去时间到……时候（已经）By the time I arrived, he had left.

✏️ 练习

练习 1：用过去完成时 (had + 过去分词) 填空
  1. After I _______________ (finish) my homework, I watched TV.
  2. She _______________ (never/be) to London before she went there last year.
  3. By the time we arrived, the movie _______________ (start).
  4. He _______________ (already/eat) when I invited him to dinner.
  5. As soon as she _______________ (leave) the house, it started to rain.
答案：
1. had finished（先做完作业 → 后看电视）
2. had never been（去年之前没去过）
3. had started（我们到的时候电影已经开始）
4. had already eaten（邀请时他已经吃过了）
5. had left（刚一离开家就开始下雨）
练习 2：选择正确的时态（过去完成时 vs 一般过去时）
  1. After he ________ (finish / had finished) his work, he ________ (go / had gone) home.
  2. I ________ (was / had been) to Paris twice before I ________ (moved / had moved) to France.
  3. When I ________ (arrived / had arrived), he ________ (already left / had already left).
  4. She ________ (said / had said) that she ________ (never saw / had never seen) such a beautiful place.
  5. We ________ (had / had had) lunch before we ________ (continued / had continued) our journey.
答案：
1. After he had finished his work, he went home.
2. I had been to Paris twice before I moved to France.
3. When I arrived, he had already left.
4. She said that she had never seen such a beautiful place.
5. We had had lunch before we continued our journey.
练习 3：改写句子（使用过去完成时避免时间混乱）
  1. First I left the village. Then I drove to the next town.
  2. First he got into the car. Then I said good morning.
  3. First she studied English. Then she went to London.
  4. First the train left. Then we arrived at the station.
  5. First he asked for a lift. Then I stopped the car.
参考答案：
1. After I had left the village, I drove to the next town.
2. As soon as he had got into the car, I said good morning.
3. She had studied English before she went to London.
4. The train had left when we arrived at the station.
5. After he had asked for a lift, I stopped the car.

🔍 特殊难点

1. ask vs ask for — "问"还是"要"？

表达含义例子
ask + 人 + 事问某人某事I asked him his name.
ask for + 物请求得到某物He asked for a lift.
ask for + 人找某人（询问）Someone asked for you.
💡 助记：ask = "问一个问题"；ask for = "想要得到某物"

2. apart from vs except — "除了"的两种含义

表达含义例子
apart from除了……（还有/之外）
= besides + except 双重含义		 Apart from a few words, I don't know French.
= except: "除了几个词以外都不会"
except除了……（排除在外） Everyone except me knew French.
除了我以外别人都会
besides除了……（还有） Besides French, he speaks English.
除了法语还会英语

3. neither of / either of / none of — 范围否定与肯定

表达范围含义例子
neither of两者（两者）都不Neither of us spoke.
either of两者（两者中）任一Either of us can help.
none of三者或以上（所有）都不None of them spoke.
💡 记忆：n- 开头 = 否定 (neither/none)；either = 二选一

4. reply vs answer

  • reply = 回答、回应（稍正式，不及物动词，需要加 to）
  • answer = 回答（及物动词，可直接加宾语）
  • He replied in the same language.（× replied me → 不能说）
  • He answered my question.（√ 可以直接说 answered my question）
