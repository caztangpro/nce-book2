📜 课文 · Text

Not for Jazz

We have an old musical instrument. It is called a clavichord. It was made in Germany in 1681. Our clavichord is kept in the living room. It has belonged to our family for a long time. The instrument was bought by my grandfather many years ago. Recently it was damaged by a visitor. She tried to play jazz on it! She struck the keys too hard and two of the strings were broken. My father was shocked. Now we are not allowed to touch it. It is being repaired by a friend of my father's.

不宜弹奏爵士乐

我们有一件古老的乐器。它叫古钢琴。它是 1681 年在德国制造的。我们的古钢琴存放在客厅里。它属于我们家已经很久了。这件乐器是我祖父多年前买来的。最近它被一位客人弄坏了。她居然用它弹爵士乐！她按键太用力，结果两根琴弦断了。我父亲非常震惊。现在我们不被允许碰它了。它正在被我父亲的一位朋友修理。

🔑 关键句型：被动语态 (Passive Voice)

什么是被动语态？

英语有主动和被动两种语态：

✅ 主动语态

主语发出动作

My grandfather bought the instrument.
→ 祖父买了乐器（主语"祖父"做动作）

🔄 被动语态

主语承受动作

The instrument was bought by my grandfather.
→ 乐器被祖父买了（主语"乐器"被动作）

💡 什么时候用被动？
① 动作的承受者比执行者更重要 → 课文 focus 的是 clavichord
② 不知道是谁做的动作 → The window was broken.（不知道谁打碎的）
③ 动作执行者很明显，不用提 → He was born in 1990.

被动语态的构成公式

be + 过去分词 (done)

be 动词根据时态变化，过去分词永远不变。
如果要说明动作的执行者，用 by + 某人。

课文中的被动语态（三种时态）

时态结构课文例子
现在时 am / is / are + done It is called a clavichord.
It is kept in the living room.
We are not allowed to touch it.
过去时 was / were + done It was made in Germany in 1681.
It was bought by my grandfather.
It was damaged by a visitor.
Two strings were broken.
My father was shocked.
现在进行时 am / is / are + being + done It is being repaired by a friend.

🔍 深层对比：课文中的主动 vs 被动

句子语态为什么？
We have an old instrument.主动主语 we 发出动作"有"
It is called a clavichord.被动它是"被叫做"——我们关注乐器本身
It was made in Germany.被动制造者不重要，关注"哪儿造的"
It was bought by my grandfather.被动仍关注乐器，但用 by 补充了执行者
She struck the keys too hard.主动她发出动作——这里她才是焦点
two strings were broken.被动关注弦"断了"这件事本身

主动语态 ↔ 被动语态的转换

主动句→ 被动句
My grandfather bought the instrument.The instrument was bought by my grandfather.
A visitor damaged it.It was damaged by a visitor.
Someone is repairing it.It is being repaired.
They don't allow us to touch it.We are not allowed to touch it.
💡 转换步骤：
1. 主动句的宾语 → 被动句的主语
2. be 动词变到与主动句动词一致的时态
3. 主动句的动词 → 过去分词
4. 主动句的主语 → by + 某人（可省略）

✏️ 练习

练习 1：标红句子是被动语态还是主动语态？
  1. The clavichord is kept in the living room.
  2. She tried to play jazz on it.
  3. Two of the strings were broken.
  4. My father was shocked.
  5. It has belonged to our family for a long time.
  6. It is being repaired by a friend.
答案：
1. 被动（is kept）
2. 主动（tried to play）
3. 被动（were broken）
4. 被动（was shocked）
5. 主动（has belonged to — belong to 是表示"属于"的状态，没有被动）
6. 被动（is being repaired）
练习 2：将主动句改为被动句
  1. Someone made this violin in Italy.
  2. My grandfather bought the clock.
  3. A naughty boy broke the window.
  4. A technician is repairing the piano.
  5. They don't allow children to enter.
答案：
1. This violin was made in Italy.
2. The clock was bought by my grandfather.
3. The window was broken by a naughty boy.
4. The piano is being repaired by a technician.
5. Children are not allowed to enter.
练习 3：用被动语态填空（注意时态！）
  1. The book _______________ (write) by Lu Xun.
  2. This house _______________ (build) in 1920.
  3. French _______________ (speak) in parts of Canada.
  4. The car _______________ (repair) at the moment.
  5. I _______________ (give) a gift on my birthday.
答案：
1. was written（过去时）
2. was built（过去时）
3. is spoken（现在时，一般事实）
4. is being repaired（现在进行时——at the moment 提示正在修）
5. was given（过去时，生日已过）
练习 4：中译英
  1. 这件乐器叫古钢琴。
  2. 古钢琴是 1681 年在德国制造的。
  3. 两根琴弦被弄断了。
  4. 我们现在不被允许碰它。
  5. 我的手机正在被修理。
参考答案：
1. The instrument is called a clavichord.
2. The clavichord was made in Germany in 1681.
3. Two strings were broken.
4. We are not allowed to touch it now.
5. My phone is being repaired.

🔍 特殊难点

1. 只有及物动词才能用于被动语态

课文中 belong to 是不及物的（后面必须跟宾语，但没有"被属于"这种说法）：

  • ✅ It belongs to our family.（主动）
  • ❌ It is belonged to our family.（没有这种被动形式）
  • 类似的不及物动词：happen, occur, seem, last, appear...

2. be shocked — 表示状态的被动

有些过去分词（如 shocked / interested / excited / bored）已变成形容词，更多是描述状态而非动作：

  • My father was shocked. → 父亲很震惊（状态）
  • I am interested in music. → 我对音乐感兴趣（状态）
  • We are excited about the trip. → 我们对旅行很兴奋（状态）

3. by 短语的省略

当动作执行者不重要、不明确或很明显时，by + 某人可以省略：

  • It was made in Germany.（谁做的？不重要）
  • The window was broken.（谁打破的？不知道或没必要说）
  • Two strings were broken.（谁弄断的？文中已有上下文）

4. a friend of my father's — 双重所有格

这是一个双重所有格 (Double Genitive)：

a friend of my father's = 我父亲的一个朋友

结构：a/an + 名词 + of + 名词所有格/物主代词

  • a book of mine = 我的一本书
  • a student of Mr. Wang's = 王老师的一个学生
  • that nose of his = 他的那个鼻子（带感情色彩）

📌 本课要点回顾

  • ✅ 课文：1681 年的古钢琴被客人弹爵士乐弄坏的故事
  • ✅ 被动语态结构：be + 过去分词 (is called / was made / is being repaired)
  • ✅ 三种时态的被动：现在时 (is/are + done)、过去时 (was/were + done)、现在进行时 (is being + done)
  • ✅ 主动→被动转换：宾语变主语 + be + 过去分词 + by + 原主语
  • ✅ 不及物动词没有被动：belong to / happen / seem 等
  • ✅ 双重所有格：a friend of my father's
