Lesson 5: No Wrong Numbers

📖 关键句型：一般过去时 vs 现在完成时（对比） |  📝 第 5 课 / 共 96 课

📜 课文 · Text

No wrong numbers

Mr. James Scott has a garage in Silbury and now he has just bought another garage in Pinhurst. Pinhurst is only five miles from Silbury, but Mr. Scott cannot get a telephone for his new garage, so he has just bought twelve pigeons. Yesterday, a pigeon carried the first message from Pinhurst to Silbury. The bird covered the distance in three minutes. Up to now, Mr. Scott has sent a great many requests for spare parts and other urgent messages from one garage to the other. In this way, he has begun his own private 'telephone' service.

没有错号

詹姆斯·斯科特先生在锡尔伯里有一个汽车修理厂，现在他刚在平赫斯特又买了一个。平赫斯特距离锡尔伯里只有五英里，但斯科特先生的新车库装不上电话，所以他刚买了12只鸽子。昨天，一只鸽子把第一封信从平赫斯特带到了锡尔伯里。这只鸟只用了三分钟就飞完了全程。到目前为止，斯科特先生从一个修理厂向另一个修理厂发送了大量索取备件的请求和其他紧急信件。就这样，他开始了自己的私人"电话"业务。

🔑 关键句型：一般过去时 vs 现在完成时

本课最关键的学习点：同一个故事中两种时态交替使用，理解为什么用这个而不是另一个。

课文时态分布

句子时态为什么？
Mr. Scott has a garage in Silbury. 一般现在时 事实陈述（他一直拥有）
He has just bought another garage. 🎯 现在完成时 刚完成的动作，与现在相关
Pinhurst is only five miles away. 一般现在时 客观事实（距离不会变）
Mr. Scott cannot get a telephone. 一般现在时 现在的状态（装不了电话）
He has just bought twelve pigeons. 🎯 现在完成时 刚完成的动作
Yesterday, a pigeon carried the first message. 🎯 一般过去时 具体过去时间（yesterday）
The bird covered the distance in 3 minutes. 🎯 一般过去时 过去的一个具体事件
Up to now, he has sent a great many requests. 🎯 现在完成时 到目前为止（up to now）
He has begun his own service. 🎯 现在完成时 已开始，结果影响现在

📌 核心判断法则

看到 具体过去时间（yesterday, last week, in 2020）→ 一般过去时

看到 just / already / never / up to now / ever / yet → 现在完成时

看到 for + 时间段 / since + 时间点 → 现在完成时

没有时间词 → 想一下：是过去的动作（一般过去时），还是与现在的联系（现在完成时）？

⚠️ 最常犯的错误：
❌ I have seen him yesterday.（yesterday 是具体过去时间，不能用现在完成时）
✅ I saw him yesterday.（一般过去时）
✅ I have seen him three times today.（today 还没结束，现在完成时）

⚡ 时态对比示例

一般过去时 ✅现在完成时 ✅
I arrived at 8 o'clock.（具体时间） I have just arrived.（刚刚到）
He bought the car last year.（去年） He has just bought a car.（刚买）
She went to Paris in 2020.（过去某年） She has been to Paris twice.（经历，不关心时间）
We lived there for five years.（现在不住了） We have lived here for five years.（现在还住）

✏️ 练习

练习 1：选择正确的时态（一般过去时或现在完成时）
  1. I _______________ (see) that film last night.
  2. She _______________ (never / be) to Japan.
  3. They _______________ (arrive) yesterday.
  4. He _______________ (just / finish) his homework.
  5. ________ you _______________ (ever / eat) Italian food?
  6. We _______________ (go) to the cinema three times last month.
  7. I _______________ (not / see) him since Monday.
  8. The pigeon _______________ (carry) the message in three minutes.
答案：
1. saw（last night → 具体时间）
2. has never been（经历）
3. arrived（yesterday → 具体时间）
4. has just finished（just → 刚刚）
5. Have ... ever eaten（经历疑问句）
6. went（last month → 已结束的时段）
7. have not seen（since → 从周一到现在）
8. carried（具体事件，in three minutes 叙述过去）
练习 2：改错（找出错误并改正）
  1. I have seen him yesterday. → _______________
  2. She has went to London. → _______________
  3. They haven't finished already. → _______________
  4. He has bought the car last week. → _______________
  5. Have you ever went to Australia? → _______________
答案：
1. I saw him yesterday.（yesterday 不能用现在完成时）
2. She has gone to London.（过去分词是 gone，不是 went）
3. They haven't finished yet.（否定句用 yet 不用 already）
4. He bought the car last week.（last week 用一般过去时）
5. Have you ever been to Australia?（gone 之后用 been，went 不对）
练习 3：中译英
  1. 我刚收到一封信。
  2. 昨天一只鸽子从平赫斯特带了一封信到锡尔伯里。
  3. 到目前为止，他已经发了大量请求。
  4. 你吃过北京烤鸭（Peking duck）吗？
  5. 我去年去过北京。
参考答案：
1. I have just received a letter.
2. Yesterday a pigeon carried a message from Pinhurst to Silbury.
3. Up to now, he has sent a great many requests.
4. Have you ever eaten Peking duck?
5. I went to Beijing last year.

🔍 特殊难点

1. another vs other

  • another = an + other，意为"又/再一个"（不定指）
    He has just bought another garage.（他又买了一个）
  • the other = 两个中的另一个（特指）
    from one garage to the other.（从一个到另一个，只有两个）
  • others = 其他人/物（复数）
    Some people like it, others don't.

2. a great many = 大量的（+ 可数名词复数）

  • a great many = a very large number of（比 a great number of 更强调数量多）
    He has sent a great many requests.
  • 后面直接跟名词，不加 of：a great many people ✅（不是 a great many of people ✘）

3. in this way = 就这样，用这种方式

  • In this way, he has begun his own private telephone service.
  • 类似的短语：by the way（顺便说一下）、in the way（挡道）、on the way（在路上）

4. "no" 用作形容词 = "一个也没有"

  • 标题 No Wrong Numbers = "没有错号"——用鸽子传信当然不会有拨错号的问题 😄
  • no + 名词 = not a / not any（更强调的语气）
    There is no telephone. = There is not a telephone.

📌 本课要点回顾

  • ✅ 课文：用鸽子代替电话传信的创意故事（"no wrong numbers"双关）
  • ✅ 一般过去时 — 具体过去时间（yesterday, last week, 3 years ago）
  • ✅ 现在完成时 — 与现在相关（just, already, never, up to now, for, since）
  • ✅ another（又一个）vs the other（另一个）
  • ✅ a great many + 可数名词复数（不加 of）
  • ✅ in this way（就这样）
