Lesson 6: Percy Buttons

📜 课文 · Text

Percy Buttons

I have just moved to a house in Bridge Street. Yesterday a beggar knocked at my door. He asked me for a meal and a glass of beer. In return for this, he stood on his head and sang songs. I gave him a meal. He ate the food and drank the beer. Then he put a piece of cheese in his pocket and went away.

Later a neighbour told me about him. Everybody knows him. His name is Percy Buttons. He calls at every house in the street once a month and always asks for a meal and a glass of beer.

珀西·巴顿斯

我刚搬到大桥街的一所房子。昨天一个乞丐来敲我的门。他向我要了一顿饭和一杯啤酒。作为回报，他倒立着唱歌。我给了他一顿饭。他吃了食物，喝了啤酒。然后他把一块奶酪放进口袋里走了。

后来一个邻居告诉了我关于他的事。大家都认识他。他叫珀西·巴顿斯。他每月一次拜访街上的每家每户，总是要一顿饭和一杯啤酒。

🔑 关键句型：冠词 a / an / the

本课最核心的知识点：什么时候用 a(n)，什么时候用 the？

课文里的冠词变化

第一次提到再次提到
I moved to a house.（你不知道是哪栋房子）—
Yesterday a beggar knocked.（你不知道这个乞丐）Later a neighbour told me about the beggar.（现在你知道这个乞丐了）
He asked for a meal and a glass of beer.（你之前不知道他要什么）He ate the food and drank the beer.（就是刚才给的那顿）
—He put a piece of cheese in his pocket.（his 代替了 the，表示已知的"他的"口袋）

核心规则

1️⃣ 第一次提到

a / an

听的人不知道你说的是什么

→
2️⃣ 第二次提到

the

听的人知道你说的是什么了

其他用 the 的情况

  • 双方都知道的东西：Please close the door.（都知道是哪扇门）
  • 独一无二的东西：the sun, the moon, the world
  • 最高级前面：the best, the most interesting
  • 序数词前面：the first, the second

用 a/an 的情况

  • 职业：He is an engineer.
  • 第一次提到的可数名词单数
  • 在感叹句 What...!：What a day!
💡 an 的规则： 后面单词以元音音素（不是字母）开头时用 an。
an hour（/aʊə/ 元音音素开头 ✅）
a university（/juː/ 辅音音素开头 ✅）
an umbrella（/ʌ/ 元音音素开头 ✅）

✏️ 练习

练习 1：填入 a / an / the，或者空着（不填）
  1. I have just bought _______ car. _______ car is very beautiful.
  2. He is _______ engineer. He works for _______ big firm.
  3. _______ sun rises in _______ east.
  4. She gave him _______ meal. He ate _______ food quickly.
  5. What _______ interesting story!
  6. _______ water is important for life.（泛指）
答案：
1. a car → The car is beautiful.（第一次→第二次）
2. an engineer → a big firm（engineer 元音开头→ an）
3. The sun rises in the east（独一无二）
4. a meal → the food quickly（第一次→第二次）
5. What an interesting story!（感叹句，interesting 以元音开头）
6. 空着（water 泛指时不可数名词不加冠词）
练习 2：改错
  1. He is a engineer.
  2. I saw the man in the street. I don't know who he was.
  3. She is the teacher. She teaches in a high school.
  4. An sun is very hot today.
  5. I ate the apple. Then I ate another apple.
答案：
1. He is an engineer.（engineer 元音开头）
2. I saw a man...（第一次提到，不知道是谁）
3. She is a teacher.（职业名词前用 a/an）
4. The sun is very hot.（太阳独一无二，用 the）
5. I ate an apple. Then I ate another apple.（第一次提到用 a/an）
练习 3：中译英
  1. 我刚搬到了一个新房子。
  2. 昨天一个人来敲我的门。
  3. 他向我要了一杯水。
  4. 他吃了那块面包，然后走了。
  5. 她是一名护士，在一家大医院工作。
参考答案：

🔍 特殊难点

1. some vs any

someany
肯定句✅ I have some money.❌
否定句❌✅ I don't have any money.
疑问句（一般）❌✅ Do you have any money?
疑问句（请求/建议）✅ Would you like some tea?❌

2. knock at vs knock down

  • knock at = 敲（门/窗）
    He knocked at my door.
  • knock down = 撞倒
    He was knocked down by a car.

3. in return for = 作为…的回报

  • In return for the meal, he stood on his head.
  • 类似表达：in exchange for（作为交换）

4. call at vs call on

  • call at = 拜访（某个地方）
    He calls at every house in the street.
  • call on = 拜访（某人）
    I called on my teacher yesterday.
