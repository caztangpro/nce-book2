Lesson 8: The Best and the Worst

📜 课文 · Text

The best and the worst

Joe Sanders has the most beautiful garden in our town. Nearly everybody enters for 'The Nicest Garden Competition' each year, but Joe wins every time. Bill Frith's garden is larger than Joe's. Bill works harder than Joe and grows more flowers and vegetables, but Joe's garden is more interesting. He has made neat paths and has built a wooden bridge over a pool. I like gardens too, but I do not like hard work. Every year I enter for the garden competition too, and I always win a little prize for the worst garden in the town!

最好的和最差的

乔·桑德斯拥有我们镇上最漂亮的花园。几乎每年大家都参加"最佳花园竞赛"，但乔每次都获胜。比尔·弗里斯的花园比乔的大。比尔比乔更勤奋，种了更多花卉和蔬菜，但乔的花园更有趣。他修了整洁的小路，还在池塘上架了一座木桥。我也喜欢花园，但我不喜欢辛苦劳动。每年我也参加花园竞赛，但我总是得个小奖——镇上最差花园！

🔑 关键句型：形容词比较级与最高级

什么是比较级和最高级？

比较级（Comparative）用于两个人或事物之间的比较，最高级（Superlative）用于三个或以上人/事物中的"最……"。

课文中的例子

原级比较级（两者比）最高级（三者及以上）
beautifulmore beautifulthe most beautiful
largelarger thanthe largest
nicenicerthe nicest
badworsethe worst
—Bill works harder than Joe.Joe has the most beautiful garden.
Bill grows more flowers than Joe.I have the worst garden.

比较级和最高级的构成规则

1️⃣ 单音节词 + -er / -est
small → smaller → the smallest
tall → taller → the tallest
hard → harder → the hardest
课文：larger, harder, nicest
2️⃣ 以 -e 结尾 + -r / -st
large → larger → the largest
nice → nicer → the nicest
fine → finer → the finest
课文：larger, nicest
3️⃣ 辅音+y → 变 y 为 i + -er/-est
busy → busier → the busiest
heavy → heavier → the heaviest
pretty → prettier → the prettiest
4️⃣ 重读闭音节 → 双写尾字母
big → bigger → the biggest
hot → hotter → the hottest
thin → thinner → the thinnest
一个元音 + 一个辅音结尾
5️⃣ 多音节词 + more / most
beautiful → more beautiful → the most beautiful
interesting → more interesting → the most interesting
课文：more beautiful, more interesting
6️⃣ 不规则变化（特殊记）
good/well → better → the best
bad/badly → worse → the worst
many/much → more → the most
little → less → the least
课文：best, worst
💡 记忆口诀：
单音节加 er / est，e 结尾只加 r / st
辅音 y 要变 i，重读闭音双写尾
多音节词 more/most 前，不规则词要记全！

比较级的重要句型

1. A + 比较级 + than + B（A 比 B 更……）

  • Bill's garden is larger than Joe's.
  • Bill works harder than Joe.
  • This garden is more interesting than that one.

2. the + 最高级 + in/of + 范围（在……中最……）

  • Joe has the most beautiful garden in our town.
  • He is the tallest in his class.
  • This is the best restaurant in the city.

注意：比较级 vs 最高级的范围词

比较级最高级
常用介词than（比……）in / of（在……中）
所比对象两者三者或以上
例子X is bigger than Y.X is the biggest in the group.
💡 常见错误：
❌ Joe has the most beautiful garden than Bill. （最高级不用 than）
✅ Joe has a more beautiful garden than Bill. （比较级才用 than）
✅ Joe has the most beautiful garden in our town. （最高级用 in/of）

练习 1：写出下列形容词的比较级和最高级
原级比较级最高级
small
big______________
busy______________
expensive______________
good______________
bad______________
答案：
big → bigger → the biggest（重读闭音节双写 g）
busy → busier → the busiest（辅音 y 变 i）
expensive → more expensive → the most expensive（多音节）
good → better → the best（不规则）
bad → worse → the worst（不规则）
练习 2：用所给词的适当形式填空
  1. This garden is _______________ (beautiful) than that one.
  2. Joe's garden is _______________ (good) in the town.
  3. Bill works _______________ (hard) than Joe.
  4. This is _______________ (bad) garden I have ever seen!
  5. My room is _______________ (small) than yours.
  6. She is _______________ (tall) girl in her class.
  7. This book is _______________ (interesting) than that one.
  8. Summer is _______________ (hot) season of the year.
答案：
1. more beautiful（多音节比较级）
2. the best（"在镇上"范围，最高级）
3. harder（单音节加 er）
4. the worst（"我见过的"范围→最高级）
5. smaller（两者比较）
6. the tallest（"在她班上"范围→最高级）
7. more interesting（多音节比较级）
8. the hottest（"一年中"范围→最高级；重读闭音节双写 t）
练习 3：中译英
  1. 我的花园比你的大。
  2. 他们在镇上最好的餐厅吃饭。
  3. 这本书比那本书有趣。
  4. 他是我们班最高的男生。
  5. 她的英语比我好。
参考答案：
1. My garden is larger than yours.
2. They eat at the best restaurant in the town.
3. This book is more interesting than that one.

练习 4：改错（找出错误并改正）
  1. She is the more beautiful girl in the class.
  2. My house is bigger that yours.
  3. He is the goodest student in the school.
  4. This car is more fast than that one.
  5. Zhangjiang is a beautiful city than Shanghai.
答案：
1. the more beautiful → the most beautiful（最高级不用 more）
2. bigger that → bigger than（"比"用 than）
3. the goodest → the best（good 是不规则变化）
4. more fast → faster（单音节词用 -er）
5. a beautiful city than → more beautiful than（比较级缺少 more）

🔍 特殊难点

1. 比较级 + than 的省略

当比较对象从上下文可以判断时，than 部分可以省略：

  • Bill works harder (than Joe).
  • I need a bigger house.（言下之意：比现在的更大）

2. than me vs than I am

  • He is taller than me.（口语、日常）
  • He is taller than I am.（书面语、更正式）
  • 两种都对，口语中 than me 更常见。

3. a little + 比较级 = "稍微……"

课文最后一句："I always win a little prize for the worst garden."

比较级也可以和 a little / much / a lot / far 配合：

  • It's a little colder today. （稍微冷一点）
  • This is much better.（好多了）
  • She is far more intelligent than him.（她比他聪明得多）
  • There are a lot more people here.（人多得多）

4. 最高级前的 the

  • 形容词最高级前 通常要加 the：the most beautiful garden
  • 副词最高级的 the 可以省略：Who works (the) hardest?
  • 做表语且不与其他人比较时可以不加 the：It's best to leave now.

5. than 前后比较对象要一致

  • ✅ Bill's garden is larger than Joe's.（花园和花园比）
  • ❌ Bill's garden is larger than Joe.（花园和人比，逻辑错误）
