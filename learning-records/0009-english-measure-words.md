# 0009 · 英语量词速查

## 日期
2026-06-15

## 来源
Lesson 6 课外交互：读到 "a piece of cheese" 后主动提问还有哪些类似量词

## 知识点
- **块状/形状**：a piece of（万能）, a slice of（薄片）, a loaf of（整条）, a bar of（条状）, a sheet of（张）
- **容器**：a glass of, a cup of, a bottle of, a can of, a bowl of
- **集合**：a group of, a pair of, a set of, a bunch of, a series of
- **微量**：a drop of, a sip of
- **抽象**：a piece of news/advice, a sense of humour, a word of warning
- **核心技巧**：piece 几乎万能不能确定时就用它
